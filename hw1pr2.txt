You will need:

    Kitchen Items-
        Butter Knife or Spoon
        Plate

    Ingredients-
        2 Slices of Bread
        Peanut Butter
        Jelly

Making The Peanut Butter and Jelly:

    Instructions-
        Step 1: Take out Kitchen Items and Ingredients
        Step 2: Place 2 Slices of Bread on Plate
        Step 3: Open Peanut Butter and Jelly
        Step 4: Use Butter Knife or spoon to get Jelly first and spread it across one slice of bread
        Step 5: Use Butter Knife or spoon to get Peanut Butter and spread on second slice of bread
        Step 6: Clean and put away Knife or spoon
        Step 7: Close and put away Peanut Butter and Jelly
        Step 8: Take both slice of bread and put them together with the ingredient side facing each other.
        Step 9: Enjoy!
